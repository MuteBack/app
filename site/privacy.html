Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

MuteBack is designed as a local-first desktop app. Its main purpose is to detect when you are speaking and lower background audio on your device.

Data collection

MuteBack does not collect personal data from you or send personal data to us. The app does not use:

  • analytics tracking
  • advertising identifiers
  • remote telemetry
  • usage profiles

Audio processing

Microphone audio is processed locally on your device for voice activity detection. MuteBack does not upload your microphone audio, background audio, settings, or voice data to a remote server.

Local settings and voice profiles

Settings are stored locally so your preferences remain available between launches. If voice enrollment is used, voice samples and the derived voice profile are also stored locally on your device.

External links

If you open external links from MuteBack or this website, those websites may have their own privacy policies and data practices.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to inbox@muteback.xyz.
